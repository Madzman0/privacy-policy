Privacy Policy for Msika Wanjala

Effective Date: 15th January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Msika Wanjala. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

By accessing or using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that identifies, relates to, describes, references, or could reasonably be linked to you ("Personal Information").

A. Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Registration: Name, email address, phone number, and password.
  • Profile Information: Profile picture, banner image, and bio/slogan.
  • Seller Information: Business details, location, payment instructions, return policies, and shipping information.
  • Verification Data: Identification documents and business registration details.
  • User Content: Product listings, images, descriptions, prices, chat messages, reviews, and ratings.

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device ID, model, OS, and platform.
  • Location Data: Precise GPS location (with permission) to show nearby shops and services.
  • Usage Data: Interactions such as searches, views, likes, and time spent in the App.
  • Network Data: IP address for security and session management.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage user accounts.
  • To process orders and facilitate communication between buyers and sellers.
  • To send notifications, alerts, and administrative messages.
  • To verify identities and prevent fraud or abuse.
  • To personalize content and recommendations.
  • To analyze usage and improve app performance.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information.

  • Between Users: Necessary information is shared to complete transactions.
  • Service Providers:
    • Cloudflare – personal and identity document storage and traffic control
    • Firebase (Google) – authentication and database services
    • Cloudinary – image storage and delivery
    • Expo – push notifications and updates
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or authorities.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy.

  • Account Deletion: Users may request account deletion within the App.
  • Deletion Process: Data is removed after a 30-day grace period, except where legally required.

6. Security of Your Information

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards such as encryption, role-based access control, and secure cloud infrastructure. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. User Eligibility

This App is intended for individuals who are legally able and prepared to engage in buying and selling activities. Users must meet any applicable age or legal requirements in their jurisdiction.
